## Who I am

I'm an engineer who has worked across every layer of the software delivery lifecycle, from writing application code, to building CI/CD infrastructure, to securing the systems that ship it.

My background spans software engineering, operations management, and cybersecurity. The work itself has ranged from development and quality engineering to platform operations and security assessment, across the full SDLC, since 2017.


## What I cover

Every piece of content on this platform comes from real-world experience:

- **Kubernetes** — cluster hardening, RBAC, admission webhooks, multi-cluster operations, and production operations beyond the official docs
- **CI/CD Pipeline Security** — GitHub Actions attack surfaces, secrets management, supply chain integrity, and securing the delivery pipeline end to end
- **GitOps** — ArgoCD, Flux, progressive delivery, and drift detection at scale
- **Infrastructure as Code** — Terraform and Ansible from first principles to production, tested and versioned
- **DevSecOps** — SAST/DAST integration, container image scanning, SBOM generation, and shift-left security in the pipeline
- **Security engineering** — workload isolation, threat modelling, and content for penetration testers and red teamers who need to understand how these systems are built and where they break

Content is structured as linked pairs — Part 1 builds the foundation, Part 2 takes it to production. The goal is always something deployable, not just something readable.
